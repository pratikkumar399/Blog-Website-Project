Those eyes were beautiful!

Author - The Champ
04 January. 6 min read


I wanted to share a story that I think , might have happened with most of you .

Every time I travel somewhere , I hope that someone very beautiful might come and seat beside me . 

Then , I even begin to dream , what will happen, when someone so beautiful will seat beside me . 


So, generally the storyline goes like ---

She comes and seats and after sometime , I introduce myself to her , then I start talking to her . She asks for my phone number , I give it to her .And then, comes the twist , even while dreaming , I go to a future state where I dream that after getting my phone number , we have started talking with each other daily . 


And then when I'm about to propose to her , an uncle of mid-age, says - " babu thoda side ho lo ".


Phir Kya , the dream remains a dream . Ab uncle ko thode na pata hai , ki main sapno me khoya hua apne babu ke bare me soch rha tha .


After that , someone enters , who is as beautiful as I thought of , but again my bad luck denies me any kind of luck . She comes and seats at a place that is invisible to me . Ab , kitna bhi gardan tedha karlo wo nhi dikhne wali ......


And the last thoughts are , "Uff! Those eyes were beautiful :)💘


So , has this happened with you also ? Every time you find someone and felt like you are in love , they vanish , with the speed of light .


 Then, you pray, if , somehow you could see that person again . Train ka pyaar, yaad to hoga hi 😆 .......


Have you ever come across such a situation? Do let me know in the comments,
