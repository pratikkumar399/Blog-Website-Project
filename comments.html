Time has flown like a wind,
since the time you met him,
since the time you left him.
But, he was still there 
standing, fighting, and making his way 
through a mountain of obstacles.

From the time he was able to understand emotions and feelings 
he started craving for it ,
from someone- 
who was unknown to him.

People came into his life just like you 
and his craving for love grew more and more .
But, they changed their paths,
unable to understand what he truly wanted.

And his search for love in someone else continued to grow 
He hated himself for not being able to love back the way he wanted,
Soon he was left with nobody to talk to,
He was in solitude with himself,
full of emotions and unanswered questions.

When, he perceived what he wanted,
He got to know the feeling of self-love,
His cravings came to an end,
after he understood the truth that 
loved are the persons who love themselves 
loved are the persons who make something of themselves 
loved are the person who have fought back and hence become Bravehearts .

From the day he got his answers 
He has no longer craved for love,
in someone else,
because it was there with him, within him.

There is a reason he is still standing,
He never knew he would be landing ,
And he will run fast,
and outlast-
everyone, that said NO!

Now, you watch him and wonder: 
How can people around him, love him,
when he once, was a person so desperate for your love? 

He has a piece of advice for you-
Go and love yourself and stop craving for his love.
